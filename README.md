Privacy Policy
Nice → Google Calendar Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: May 27, 2026

Overview
Nice → Google Calendar is a Chrome extension that reads your work schedule from the NICE WFM WebStation and converts it into a downloadable ICS file for import into Google Calendar. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Do Not Collect
This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data — personal or otherwise. Specifically, we do not collect:

Personally identifiable information (name, email address, employee ID, etc.)
Authentication credentials or session tokens
Browsing history or web activity
Location data
Financial or health information
Any usage analytics or telemetry
How the Extension Works
When you click the Export This Week button, the extension reads the schedule table displayed on your currently open NICE WFM page (toast.nicecloudsvc.com). This data is processed entirely within your browser to produce an ICS calendar file, which is then saved directly to your computer.

No schedule data, no personal information, and no other content from the page is ever sent to any external server, database, or third party. Everything happens locally on your device.

Permissions
The extension requests the following Chrome permissions, each used solely to perform the export:

activeTab — to read the schedule from your open NICE WFM tab.
scripting — to inject the schedule-reading script if needed.
downloads — to save the generated ICS file to your computer.
Host access to toast.nicecloudsvc.com — to access the schedule page where your shift data is displayed.
Third-Party Services
This extension does not integrate with, communicate with, or transmit data to any third-party service, API, or analytics platform.
